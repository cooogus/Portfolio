Fancy Bear Goes Phishing

by Scott J. Shapiro · August 10, 2025

Rating ★★★★☆
Started July 27, 2025 · Finished August 10, 2025
StoryGraph

I don't love to read super technical books in my free time primarily because they can get so bogged down in the details that reading becomes working more than leisure, but Shapiro does a great job at treating his reader well.

Shapiro's background as a Law Professor allows him to bring a unique background to the table in this ever evolving discussion. The book jumps through a bunch of case studies and taught me many new things without ever feeling like I was sitting in a lecture hall.
