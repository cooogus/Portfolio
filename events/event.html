Day Trip to San Francisco

San Francisco, CA · May 25, 2024

I headed to San Francisco with a few friends—a quick Bay Area escape beyond our Davis routine. We wandered neighborhoods, grabbed photos, and soaked up perfect weather.

We try to get out every once in a while to see more of the region. It’s always fun to trade the usual coffee shops for something new and recharge together.
